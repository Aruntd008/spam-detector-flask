Compare Spam Detection Models

Note: This will train multiple models on the same dataset and compare their performance. This process may take several minutes to complete. Make sure you have loaded your dataset first.
Models that will be compared:
  • Traditional ML: Logistic Regression, Naive Bayes, Random Forest
  • Gradient Boosting: XGBoost, LightGBM
  • Ensemble Methods: Voting Classifier, Stacking Classifier
  • Deep Learning: LSTM with Word Embeddings, CNN with Word Embeddings

BERT is excluded from automatic comparison due to its resource intensity.

Warning: This operation is computationally intensive and may take a while to complete.
What will be compared:
  • Accuracy: Overall correct predictions
  • Precision: Accuracy of spam predictions
  • Recall: Ability to find all spam emails
  • F1 Score: Balance between precision and recall
  • Training Time: Time taken to train the model
  • Model Efficiency: Performance relative to time
